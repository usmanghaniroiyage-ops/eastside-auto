Five quiet
losses.
One fix.

None of them feel catastrophic on their own. Together they're draining thousands from garages across the UK every year — and most owners never trace it back to the source.

01

The call you couldn't take

You're under a bonnet. The phone rings four times. The caller doesn't leave a voicemail — 85% never do. They Google the next garage and book with them. You never knew the call happened.

How Risora fixes it

Risora answers within two rings. It sounds like your receptionist, captures the caller's details, and pings your WhatsApp before you put the spanner down.

02

The booking no one confirmed

A customer books in. No message arrives. They assume it fell through and don't show. Your bay sits empty for two hours on a day you could have filled it.

How Risora fixes it

The moment a booking is made, Risora sends a WhatsApp confirmation. Then again at 24 hours, 1 hour, and 15 minutes. Customers reply CONFIRM or RESCHEDULE.

03

The no-show that never comes back

The appointment time passes. Nobody arrives. The bay is wasted. Without a follow-up, the customer is often too embarrassed to rebook and quietly goes elsewhere.

How Risora fixes it

Fifteen minutes after a missed appointment, Risora sends a "we missed you" WhatsApp. Most rebook the same day. Bay refilled. Customer kept.

04

The MOT customer who forgot you

A garage doing 1,000 MOTs a year typically loses 150–250 of those customers the following year. They didn't leave unhappy. They just forgot where they went.

How Risora fixes it

Risora tracks every MOT date via the DVLA's public API. At 30, 14, and 3 days before expiry, customers get a WhatsApp reminder. One tap and they're booked in with you.

05

The complaint that went straight online

A frustrated customer calls twice. No answer. They don't try again — they leave a 1-star review instead. You find out three weeks later when your ranking drops.

How Risora fixes it

Risora spots the pattern — multiple unanswered calls from the same number — and flags it to your WhatsApp before the review goes up. You can reply from the same number.
